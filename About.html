About

Our story

Our product designer, Ali had struggled with body odor for years, trying different products without success. One day, he stumbled upon a local attar shop and was intrigued by attar made from natural ingredients. After trying a sample, he loved it's smell, providing him with more confidence and less self-consciousness about his body odor. He spent countless hours experimenting with different ingredients until he developed a line of attars that he was proud of. With Pragnesh Gandhi, they created Attar Souk, that offers high-quality, natural attars. Attar Souk's attars are long-lasting and pure. We have numerous stories of how Attar Souk has improved their lives. For instance, Aisha, who had struggled with stress and anxiety, found that Attar Souk's natural fragrances helped her relax and feel more grounded. Ahmed, who was seeking a fragrance to wear during worship, was amazed by how long the scent lasted and how it helped him focus and feel more connected to his spirituality.
